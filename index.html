Fenris

Fenris is a nimble and merciless Hunter, best suited to mid-range combat. His equipment enables him to ambush his enemies and escape in the blink of an eye.

Trenchwalker

A Hunter with a combat medic loadout, he excels at close-to-medium range combat. His Leech Gun and Hybrid Grenade can heal both himself and his squadmate.

Weaver

A defensive Hunter ideally suited for mid-range combat. While behind the cover of his Energy Barrier, Weaver can deal immense damage with his Minigun.
